# Privacy Policy for TurnTimer

TurnTimer does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app uses the device microphone only to detect the spoken word “Next” in order to change the active player during gameplay.  
All audio processing is performed locally on the device. No audio recordings are saved or transmitted.

TurnTimer does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or third-party SDKs.
